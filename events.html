Events in GIT under IEEE Student Branch

In 2004 IEEE STB had the First Inter-College Annual Event INFINITY.

2006 onwards IEEE-GIT started its annual flagship event Paanchajanya(PCH), an event which attracted Delegates from Sangli, Kolhapur, Goa, Bengaluru and Hyderabad.
PCH 2015 celebrated 30yrs of initiation of a Student chapter at GIT. (20yrs of being active since 1995-96) and 10yrs of PCH Dr. D H Rao, Prof. Hemant Kamat, Dr. Raghavendra V Kulkarni, Dr. G R Udupi and Prof V P Gejji were felicitated.

It is a whole day event with the most interesting activities which improves communication skills, helps participants to become a social person. This event also helps in business as well as marketing skills through the activities like product designing and selling the self-made product.
This event helps in overall personality development of an individual.

IEEE SB was a part of NK Hub-level Event i-GREEN organized in 2014, with the Theme of Global Warming.

The WIE Affinity group IEEE Student branch celebrates Women's Day since 2003. It was initially called, TRIGARTI now known as "UDAAN"!
The Panel discussion theme was “Careers beyond Engineering”.
Flash mob, Fun events like one-minute games, food stalls, musical chairs etc. were held. Felicitation of non-teaching staff was also done. The Panelists invited were:
  • Ms. Manjiri Tarcar(Caterer, Goa)
  • Ms. Jyotsana Pai (Pai Foods, Belagavi)
  • Ms. Amruta Redekar (Technifix Pvt. Ltd, Mumbai)
  • Ms. Deepti Desai (Photographer, Banglore).

IEEE Day, an event with the Theme "The Art of Writing Technical Paper", was conducted.
