Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) - GIT

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers is a professional association for electronic engineering and electrical engineering with its corporate office in New York City and its operations center in Piscataway, New Jersey.

History of IEEE in KLS GIT

IEEE-GIT is an active student branch of Karnatak Law Society's Gogte Institute of Technology, Belgaum, and is one of the first and the largest student branches in India.

  • The IEEE Student branch was initiated in the year 1995-96.
  • In June 2003, global IEEE allotted our college the code STB28191.
  • It was initiated by the team of academicians Dr. Raghavendra V. Kulkarni, Mr. Hemant Kamat of Shalaka Enterprises, Pune (then professor at EC GIT), Dr. D. H. Rao and Dr. Baliga
  • IEEE Student branch is the first professional student chapter of KLS's GIT.
  • Dr. D H Rao was the first Branch Counselor from 2003-2009.
  • Followed by the membership of Dr.Santosh Saraf, Prof. Vineeta Gejji and Dr. Veena Desai.
  • Second branch counselor – Dr Vineeta Gejji 2009-2019.
  • Current branch counselor: Prof. Abhishek Deshmukh.

Achievements

  • One of the oldest student branches of North Karnataka region with an history of over two decades.
  • Global IEEE had allotted funds to conduct the “star program” in the year 2014-2015 for being one of the most active chapter in the region, using these funds we conducted motivational events in rural areas (i.e. the government schools).
  • For the year 2015-2016 Vardhana Kulkarni and Pranali Shinde from KLS GIT were selected as representatives for North Karnataka region.
  • Student Branch got seed amount in 2015-16 for IEEE Star program.
  • Three of our professors Dr. D.V.Kulkarni, Dr. V P Gejji and Prof. N.F.Deshpande were elected as senior members of IEEE.
